AdGuard Content Blocker is an app that will block all ads <b>only in Yandex Browser and Samsung Internet browser</b> without requiring you to root your device. AdGuard Content Blocker <b>doesn't block</b> ads in any other apps.
Customize the app to suit your needs - choose any from more than 20 language-specific and general filtering rules lists available to you.

The app is open source and can be found on GitHub:
https://github.com/AdGuardTeam/ContentBlocker
